RunWalk Coach Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Data Stored

RunWalk Coach stores workout sessions, training settings, and, when you sign in with Google, the Google account identifier and email address needed to save progress on the server. Workout reports can include manually entered heart-rate values, perceived difficulty, pain, breathing level, and notes.

Local Storage

The app stores workout sessions and settings in your browser's storage so the app can work without login. If browser storage, site data, cookies, or cache are cleared, local-only progress can be lost. When you are signed in with Google, supported progress can sync to the server.

Google Login

Google OAuth is used only to identify your account for server sync. RunWalk Coach does not store a Google password.

Deletion

You can delete browser progress from Settings. When signed in with Google, deletion also removes the server profile and related workout sessions, template overrides, and auth sessions for that account.

Contact

For privacy and support questions, contact support@storycraftbooks.com.
